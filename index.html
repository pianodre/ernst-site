About Me

With 16 years of piano experience and a passion for music education, I provide personalized instruction tailored to each student's goals and learning style. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your technique, I'm here to guide you on your musical journey.

I believe that learning piano should be enjoyable and rewarding. My teaching philosophy combines classical technique with practical skills, ensuring students develop a strong foundation while exploring music they love. Each year we celebrate progress with a studio-wide recital every May.

  • 16 years of piano experience
  • Certificate of Merit graduate
  • Teaching since 2019
  • All ages and skill levels welcome
